Biryani

A delicious indian dish that contains rice mixed with spices!

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the people of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with spices, rice, and meat (usually chicken, goat, lamb, prawn, fish or beef), or eggs or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. Biryani is the single most-ordered dish on Indian online food ordering andf delivery services.
